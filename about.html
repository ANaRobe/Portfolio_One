A summary about my work experience


During my studies (SNSPA Bucharest - BA degree in European Public Administration) I had to face the challenges of moving to the USA to gain professional experience there. I took every step independently - from applying for a visa, deciding on an employment contract, organizing the trip, accommodating and learning about immigration obligations, to protecting my rights. That was just the beginning because 3 years later I followed the same steps but in a completely new system whose official language I didn't know at the time. I've now completed the integration course in Germany and speak four languages ​​fluently and have a liking for another four. As a field worker at the Studio Moderna call center, I used CRM as a marketing tool to manage information and organize databases. At the annual Autodesk event, I proved that I have strong organizational, assertive, and multitasking skills. I was also able to use these skills in organizing excursions for the Carpathian Tourism Agency. At the same time, I improved my communication and presentation skills there. Through my many years of work in the areas of trade and tourism, I have developed a strong service orientation. I'm also used to handling bureaucratic processes.
